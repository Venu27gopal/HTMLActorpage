Vijay is my hero

Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam,[151][152] a Sri Lankan Tamil whom he met in the United Kingdom, on 25 August 1999.[153][154] They have two children.[155] Vijay's son, Jason Sanjay, made a cameo appearance with his father in Vettaikaaran (2009)[156] and his daughter portrayed a small role as her father's pre-teen daughter in the climax of Theri (2016).
